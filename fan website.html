A Little about Damon

Damon Salvatore is a character in the show called The Vampire Diaries. His character has a brother named Stefan Salvatore and they both have the same love interest. Her name is Elena Gilbert I have watched this show countless of times and have grown to love the character Damon and I love him with Elena.

How Damon Acts

Damon is a very passionate guy who doesn't let anyone get in the way of him. When he loves someone he loves hard. He has a very distinct personality and is very protective of what he loves. He is definently a romantic and he is sometimes described to be sinister, dangerous, generally unkind, uncaring, cold hearted, ruthless and unsympathetic. But to me I see him only act like that with people he really doesn't like. He can actually be a very good guy and can sometimes have a good heart. Hes very good with his brother who he loves very much and would do anything for.

How Damon's Relationship with his Brother is

Damon and his brother share a very special relationship considering they have both been vampires for an extremely long time together. They have both been through so much including many ups and downs. Sometimes they would go decades without eachother because they could and they got mad with eachother. Other times they can be inseperable but overall at the end of the day they always forgave eachother and were there for eachother. They had a great brother bond.
