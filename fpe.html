Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: Phantom Blood

Jojo's Bizarre adventure began as a manga series that spawned two anime versions.
In part one of the series, you are introduced to Jonathan Joestar, the first in a continuing line of empowered characters within the Joestar family
Jonathan becomes the nemesis of Dio Brando, who becomes a vampire and eventually succeeds in killing him.

Many years later, the grandson of Jonathan Joestar, Joseph, faces a resurrected Dio Brando and the legacy of the Joestar conflict with Dio continues.
